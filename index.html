About Us

Specialized Agroforestry Services (SAS) focuses on integrating agroforestry practices into Carbon Credit projects to create sustainable and profitable solutions for landowners.

Our mission is to reduce poverty, protect the environment, and enhance agricultural productivity through innovative methods that combine tree husbandry with farming practices.

  • Long-term Income: Tree husbandry provides a steady income stream while protecting the environment.
  • Soil Protection: Trees prevent erosion and improve soil health.
  • Sustainable Synergies: Combining trees with cash crops creates multiple revenue streams and ensures sustainability.
